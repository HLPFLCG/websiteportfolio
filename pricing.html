Pricing Questions

Are there any hidden fees?

No! The price you see is the price you pay. No surprise costs, no hidden fees. You'll only need to pay separately for domain ($10-15/year) and hosting ($5-50/month).

What if I need more than 10 pages?

No problem! Additional pages can be added for a small fee. Most businesses find 10 pages is plenty to start, and you can always add more later.

Do you offer discounts?

Yes! Save 5% by paying in full upfront. We also offer referral bonuses if you refer other businesses to us.

What's included in the 30 days of support?

Bug fixes, minor adjustments, technical support, and answering any questions you have. After 30 days, you can maintain the site yourself or hire us for ongoing support.

Can I upgrade my package later?

Absolutely! You can start with the Essential package and add logo design or branding services later.

What if I'm not satisfied?

We include revision rounds to ensure you're happy with the result. We'll work with you until it's right. Your satisfaction is our priority.
