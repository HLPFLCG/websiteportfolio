# HLPFL Website - Future Enhancement Suggestions

## Priority 1: High Impact, Quick Wins

### 1. Social Media Integration
**Effort:** Low | **Impact:** High
- Connect actual social media accounts to footer links
- Add social sharing buttons to portfolio items
- Integrate Instagram feed on homepage
- Add LinkedIn company page link

### 2. Analytics Setup
**Effort:** Low | **Impact:** High
- Implement Google Analytics or Plausible
- Track user behavior and conversions
- Set up goal tracking for contact form submissions
- Monitor page performance metrics

### 3. SEO Enhancements
**Effort:** Medium | **Impact:** High

**Effort:** Low | **Impact:** Medium
