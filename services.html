Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it really take?

If you have all your content, images, and branding ready, we can deliver in 72 hours. Most projects take 1-2 weeks because we're working together to gather materials and make refinements.

What if I don't like the design?

We include revision rounds to ensure you're happy. We'll work with you until it's right. Your satisfaction is our priority.

Can I update the website myself later?

Yes! We'll provide training on how to make basic updates. Or we can handle updates for you with our maintenance packages.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer 50/50 payment split and can discuss custom arrangements that work for your budget.

What if I need more than 10 pages?

No problem! We can quote additional pages separately. Most businesses find 10 pages is plenty to start.

Do you provide hosting?

We don't provide hosting directly, but we'll recommend reliable, affordable hosting providers and help you set everything up.
