HLPFL.org - Music Business Services Platform

Helping independent musicians build legally solid businesses with standout brands

The Challenge

HLPFL needed a comprehensive platform to showcase their music business services while establishing credibility in a competitive industry. The website needed to communicate complex services clearly while appealing to creative professionals.

The Solution

We created a modern, musician-friendly design with bold visuals and clear service communication. The website features a statistics dashboard showcasing their success (500+ careers, 98% success rate, $50M+ protected), a timeline visualization of company growth, and a resource center positioning HLPFL as an industry authority.

Key Features

  • Service showcase (Legal Foundation, Artist Branding, Music Rights)
  • Founder profile and company story
  • Statistics dashboard with impressive metrics
  • Timeline visualization of company growth
  • Resource center for musicians
  • Mobile-optimized for on-the-go musicians
