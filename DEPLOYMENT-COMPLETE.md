## ğŸ“ Key Learnings & Best Practices

### What Worked Well
1. **Conversion-focused design** - Clear CTAs and trust signals
2. **Sliding scale pricing** - Appeals to budget-conscious founders
3. **Founder story** - Builds credibility and relatability
4. **Fast delivery promise** - 72-hour guarantee is compelling
5. **US-based work** - Strong differentiator from competitors
6. **Transparent process** - 4-step process reduces uncertainty

### Design Decisions
1. **Copper/Teal color scheme** - Professional yet approachable
2. **Large typography** - Bold headlines grab attention
3. **Floating particles** - Adds visual interest without distraction
4. **Card-based layout** - Easy to scan and digest
5. **Accordion FAQs** - Reduces page length while providing info
6. **Gradient effects** - Modern and premium feel

### Technical Choices
1. **Inline CSS/JS** - Faster loading, no external dependencies
2. **Mobile-first** - Ensures great experience on all devices
3. **Lightweight files** - Fast loading times improve conversions
4. **Semantic HTML** - Better SEO and accessibility
5. **Progressive enhancement** - Works without JavaScript
