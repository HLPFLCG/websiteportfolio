## üìù BEST PRACTICES

### Performance:
- ‚úÖ Compress all images (TinyPNG, ImageOptim)
- ‚úÖ Use WebP format with JPG fallback
- ‚úÖ Lazy load images below the fold
- ‚úÖ Optimize videos (< 5MB for background videos)
- ‚úÖ Use CDN for faster delivery

### Accessibility:
- ‚úÖ Always include alt text for images
- ‚úÖ Provide video captions/transcripts
- ‚úÖ Ensure sufficient color contrast
- ‚úÖ Don't rely solely on images to convey information

### Brand Consistency:
- ‚úÖ Use brand colors (copper #c87941, teal #2c5f6f)
- ‚úÖ Maintain consistent style across all visuals
- ‚úÖ Choose images that match brand personality
- ‚úÖ Apply consistent filters/overlays
